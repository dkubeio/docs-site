# FAQ

## The canvas is blank after login.
Refresh the page. If the issue persists, confirm your DKubeX session is still valid from the profile menu in the header.

## My component shows a red error badge.
Click the component to open its side panel and scroll to the **Logs** section. Most errors are missing API keys or invalid field values.

## How do I switch between light and dark mode?
Open the profile dropdown in the top-right of the header and pick **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** from the theme toggle.

## Where are my flows stored?
Flows live inside DKubeX projects. You can organize them into folders and share a project with other DKubeX users from the project settings.

## How do I roll back to a previous version of a flow?
Open the flow menu (pencil icon next to the flow name) and choose **Version history**.

## Can I self-host?
Yes. See the upstream Langflow project for self-hosting instructions. The DKubeX integration adds the unified header and deployment workflow described here.
