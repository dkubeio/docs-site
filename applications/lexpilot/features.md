# Features

LexPilot automates legal due diligence for corporate and securities transactions. It reads your deal documents, extracts the important details, checks them for problems, and produces a clear report — replacing hours of manual review with minutes of automated analysis.

## Document understanding

- **Document classification** — automatically recognizes the type of each uploaded file, such as term sheets, stock purchase agreements, board resolutions, articles of incorporation, and subscription agreements.
- **Text extraction** — reads both digital and scanned PDF documents.
- **Field extraction** — pulls the key details out of every document — parties, dates, dollar amounts, valuations, share counts, and more — and records a confidence level for each value.

## Multi-agent review

Each deal package is reviewed by a team of specialized agents, each responsible for one part of the job:

- **Lex** — sorts and classifies the incoming documents.
- **Petra** — extracts the text and key fields.
- **Gavel** — checks the documents against each other for consistency.
- **Reese** — analyzes the contracts for risks, obligations, and unusual terms.
- **Portia** — checks the deal against regulatory requirements.

## Consistency & issue detection

LexPilot catches problems early, before they reach closing:

- Entity names that don't match across documents.
- Dollar amounts and share counts that disagree.
- Conflicting or impossible dates.
- Required documents that are missing for the transaction type.
- Governance gaps, such as an undersized board.

Every issue is graded by severity — critical, warning, or informational — so the most important items stand out.

## Compliance checking

- More than 50 built-in regulatory rules covering California and Delaware corporate law, SEC Regulation D, California securities law, the HSR Act, and corporate governance best practices.
- Rules are applied automatically based on each matter's transaction type, jurisdiction, and deal value.
- Each matter receives an overall compliance score with a clear breakdown by category.

## Reports

- Comprehensive PDF deal reports covering the summary, document inventory, extracted details, issues, compliance results, and recommendations.
- A complete, timestamped history of every document, change, and review step.

## Notifications

- Real-time alerts when a review finishes or a critical issue is found.

## Transaction types

LexPilot supports the common corporate and securities transactions, including:

- Venture capital financings
- Mergers and acquisitions
- Company formations
- Securities offerings
- Debt financings
